How It Works: Under the Hood

Mempool & Blockchain Scanning

Our distributed network of nodes connects directly to EVM-compatible chains, scanning the mempool for pending transactions and analyzing newly mined blocks in real-time. This gives us a comprehensive view of all contract creation and interaction events as they happen.

Bytecode & Heuristic Analysis

Each new contract's bytecode is analyzed against a vast library of known scam patterns, vulnerabilities, and malicious functions. We use advanced heuristics to detect suspicious characteristics like hidden minting functions, disabled selling mechanisms (honeypots), and excessive transaction fees.

AI-Powered Threat Modeling

We leverage a proprietary machine learning model trained on millions of data points from both legitimate and fraudulent contracts. This model identifies novel threats and sophisticated scams by recognizing subtle patterns and correlations that would be missed by static analysis alone.

Real-time Scoring & Alerting

Contracts and transactions are assigned a dynamic risk score based on our multi-layered analysis. High-risk events are immediately flagged and pushed to our data stream, allowing you to integrate real-time threat intelligence directly into your applications via our API.
